ADAPTIVE CACHE MANAGEMENT USING PREDICTIVE ACCESS MODELS

Abstract: A memory subsystem in which cache line residency is governed by a
predictive model of future access patterns. The model is trained on historical
access traces and produces, for each resident line, an estimated time until
next reference. Eviction candidates are selected in descending order of the
estimate.

Claim 1. A cache controller comprising: a trace buffer recording memory access
events; a prediction unit generating, for each cached entry, an estimate of
time-to-next-access; and an eviction unit selecting for replacement the entry
having the largest estimate.

Claim 2. The controller of claim 1, wherein the prediction unit is retrained
on a periodic schedule without interrupting cache service.

Claim 3. The controller of claim 2, wherein eviction is suspended when the
